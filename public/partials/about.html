We're foodful

Foodful is a simple platform that connects restaurants and business to food banks and charities. If you’re not a business or charity, feel free to peruse our sampled database of food banks in America, either by using that there search bar on the top right corner or by visiting feedingamerica.com.

Foodful keeps track of a restaurant’s donations, so feel free to show off your profile and make other restaurants jealous, if you’re into that. Oh and food donations are tax deductible, so you’ll make some coin while helping other people out - isn’t that awesome?
